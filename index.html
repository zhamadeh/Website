--- layout: home title: Zeid Hamadeh subtitle: PhD Candidate, Athlete, Tutor, Coach, Artist --- I'm a Vancouver-based computational biologist with a PhD in Genome Science from the University of British Columbia. My scientific interests include cancer genomics, genetic sequencing and bioinformatics.


My doctoral dissertation focused on exploring the ways in which DNA repair is perturbed in cancer using single-cell genomic sequencing. This work primarily focused on addressing the need for tools that can uncover the incredible amount of genetic diversity that exists within cancer, being largely a genetic disease. My other interests and pursuits include competing in Canada's top beach volleyball tour after having played 5 years of varsity indoor volleyball during my undergraduate and graduate studies. I also work with kids in a few different settings, mostly coaching, tutoring and science outreach.
